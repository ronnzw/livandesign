Interiors: Active Gym, Borrowdale Race Course

Designing a fully immersive experience that can change from a dynamic branded setting to a state-of-the-art exercise facility was the brief for the room.

Reflective surfaces and textures are present throughout the gym, aiming the play with the sunlight that projects through the windows. Lighting fixtures give emphasis to the raised ceilings, creating a sense of luxury. A notable aspect of the design is how each space's lighting has been thoughtfully encompassed. These lights produce evocative ambiances that interact with the club's textured surfaces, enveloping patrons in a heavenly experience during their workout sessions. It is decorated with gentle textures, warm and soothing tones, and a balanced neutral palette.

This wellness suite is intended to be a devoted haven for people looking to unwind and revitalize themselves.
