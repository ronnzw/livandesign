Carrick Creagh

Sitting atop the cliff's edge, this serene property boasts spectacular views of the surrounding nature. The boundary dividing these vistas from the interior is figuratively broken by floor-to-ceiling windows throughout–creating a sense of openness and fluidity between nature and the home. This home embraces the grandeur of nature's beauty, it offers a lifestyle that appreciates modernism, nature, and the art of living well.

These dynamically piled volumes create an eye-catching structure on top of a steep slope. This residence features panoramic views that encapsulate the essence of its breathtaking surroundings, creating a picturesque backdrop. At the back of this masterpiece is an entertainment area that accommodates the pool, poolside sitting area and a fire bowl, which encourages outdoor living.

This is evidence that in today’s world, architects are pushing the boundaries and creating structures that withstand extreme weather conditions, steep, rocky terrain, and limited accessibility, all while working with the land.
