Chisipite residential home

Perched atop Chisipite’s hills is a high-end private residence, designed as an oasis for luxury contemporary living. This modern home is a stunning example of how architecture can push the boundaries of what a home can be.

The design was conceptualised as stacked and intersecting ‘boxes’, which rest on top of each other and intersecting monolithic concrete clad walls. Drawing inspiration from the timeless allure of contemporary aesthetic, the dwelling is meticulously crafted to marry modern sophistication with rustic charm. Here, clean lines and sleek finishes converge with warm, inviting interiors, creating a sanctuary of comfort and elegance

In order to counterbalance the imposing façade, warmth and natural hues were incorporated into the interior design of the house. Notable features include open-plan layout, interconnecting the exterior and the interior, bathed in an abundance of natural light.

Its unique design and functional features make it not only aesthetically pleasing but also energy-efficient and sustainable, setting a new standard in luxurious residential living.
