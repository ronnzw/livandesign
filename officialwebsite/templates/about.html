Who are we?

We are a modern architectural design and urban planning firm located in Harare, Zimbabwe, that specializes in residential and commercial projects across the country. Livan Design Innovation supplies creative solutions that are customized to suit each client’s personal requirements. We pride ourselves on applying our skills to realise our clients’ ambitions and dreams whilst reducing the running costs through energy efficient design resolutions. Through the years, we have worked with many developers, owners and contractors, providing an array of services.
